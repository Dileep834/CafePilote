# Reports & Analytics Module

## 1. Executive Dashboard & Metrics
- **Sales Analytics**: Gross Sales, Net Sales, Tax Collected, Discounts Given, Average Ticket Value (ATV).
- **Product Performance**: Top 10 Bestsellers, Slow-moving inventory, Category distribution pie charts.
- **Shift & Cash Drawer Audit**: Discrepancy logs between calculated system balance and physical cash counted.
- **Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)**: Profitability analysis combining recipe cost against sale price.

## 2. Export Formats
- **CSV / Excel**: Raw tabular data for accounting software import (Tally, QuickBooks, Zoho Books).
- **PDF Reports**: Stylized executive summaries with header logos and visual charts.
