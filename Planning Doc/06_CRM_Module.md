# CRM & Customer Loyalty Module

## 1. Features & Capabilities
- **Customer Profiles**: Track name, phone number, email, birthday, anniversary, and visit history.
- **Loyalty Points Engine**: Earn X points per currency amount spent; redeem points during POS checkout.
- **Tiered Memberships**: Silver, Gold, Platinum tiers with automatic tier upgrades based on lifetime spend.
- **Targeted Promotions**: Segment customers by favorite items or inactivity to send discount codes.

## 2. Loyalty Calculation Formula
$$\text{Points Earned} = \lfloor \text{Eligible Order Subtotal} \times \text{Tier Multiplier} \rfloor$$
$$\text{Redemption Value} = \text{Points} \times \text{Point-to-Currency Exchange Rate}$$
