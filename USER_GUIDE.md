# CafePilots - Operations User Guide

Welcome to the CafePilots platform! This guide explains how to use the core features of the system depending on your role.

## 1. Roles and Security Levels
*   **Super Admin / Brand Admin**: Has full access to everything. Can view master lists across all franchises, create new companies, manage recipes, and view system settings.
*   **Franchise Manager / Owner**: Can view the dashboard for their specific franchise. They can manage sales, view stock, process purchase orders, and see the supplier list. They cannot edit recipes or user accounts.
*   **Staff**: Only has access to operations (Sales Entry, Current Stock, Daily Update, Waste). They cannot see settings, suppliers, or master pages.

## 2. Managing Recipes (Bill of Materials)
*Only available to Admins.*
1. Navigate to **Masters > Recipes**.
2. Click **Create Recipe**.
3. Select a Finished Product (e.g., "Peri Peri Burger").
4. Add all the Raw Materials required to make it (e.g., "Burger Bun", "Cheese").
5. Save. This links the raw materials to the finished product for automatic POS deductions.

## 3. Point of Sale (Logging Sales)
*Available to Staff and Managers.*
1. Navigate to **Sales Entry**.
2. Select the items a customer ordered (e.g., 2 Lattes, 1 Burger) and add them to the cart.
3. Click **Process Sale**.
4. **Automation**: The system will automatically look up the Recipes you created and deduct the exact amount of raw materials (milk, coffee beans, buns) from your current stock.

## 4. Supplier Management
*Available to Managers and Admins.*
1. Navigate to **Masters > Suppliers**.
2. Keep an active directory of all your vendors, categorizing them by the goods they supply (Dairy, Meat, Packaging) alongside their contact information.

## 5. System Configuration
*Available to Managers and Admins.*
1. Navigate to **Settings**.
2. Configure your cafe's closing times, grace periods, and brand logos here. Your configurations are saved instantly.
